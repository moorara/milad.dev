**Values**

  - 💡 Integrity
  - 💡 Pragmatism
  - 💡 Empathy
  - 💡 Respect

**Do's**

  - ✅ Automate
  - ✅ Simplify and clean
  - ✅ Make it easy to understand, maintain, and collaborate
  - ✅ Start with UX/DX
  - ✅ Build just enough
  - ✅ Fix the root cause
  - ✅ Make lasting changes
  - ✅ Treat other developers like customers

**Don'ts**

  - ❌ Over-engineering
  - ❌ Over-abstraction
  - ❌ Leaky abstractions
  - ❌ Premature optimization
