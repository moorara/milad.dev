---
title: "My Manifesto"
date: 2019-10-02T22:00:00-04:00
---

**Do's**

  - ✅ Simplify and clean
  - ✅ make it easy to understand, maintain, and collaborate
  - ✅ Start with UX/DX
  - ✅ Build just enough
  - ✅ Fix the root cause
  - ✅ Make lasting changes
  - ✅ Treat other developers like customers

**Don'ts**

  - ❌ Over-engineering
  - ❌ Over-abstraction
  - ❌ Leaky abstractions
  - ❌ Premature optimization
